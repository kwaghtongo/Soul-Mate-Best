About Soul Mate Global Enterpreise

Soul Mate Global Enterprise is a high technological enterprise that provides services in Information and Communication Technology, supply of Mining Equipment, Pharmaceutical Products and industrial goods.

WHAT WE DO IN ICT.
We have well experience ICT professional that develop and design, web and desktop application both the front end and the back end, create and manage database as well as project management, and full range of technology to meet our customer need. We sale and supply ICT equipments including hardware and software.
WHAT WE DO IN MINING.
We supply Mining equipment, both New and used once, such as magnetic separators, excavators, crushers, different types of screen, classifiers, grinders, flotation equipments etc. Our products have a good reputation in Nigeria, Ghana, Chad, Kenya, South Africa and other African countries.

OUR PHARMACEUTICAL PRODUCTS.
We supply pharmaceutical products such as pet bottles of different size, gelatin capsules, personal protective equipments, hospital equipments etc.
